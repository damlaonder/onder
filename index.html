about __________________________________________________________________

I’m a Turkish-American woman finishing my Bachelor’s in Computer Science at New York University. I grew up moving around different schools in West Virginia and Tennessee, developing a love for reading in communities with low accessibility to educational resources, leading me to want to help foster a love of learning and reading for future generations. I plan on doing that by using software development to make books and education more accessible.

In school, I have learned how to develop and implement strong software programs with databases to efficiently share information. Outside of school, I have been interning at Start Lighthouse, a non-profit determined to closing the literacy gap, and working as an Instructor with CodeAdvantage, a company determined to create learning environments filled with both creative exploration and logical thinking.
