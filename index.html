about

I’m Damla, a Turkish-American woman with dreams to make education more accessible!

I have a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science from New York University, where I also dedicated two years to studying in the Liberal Arts Core Program. During my time as a student, I had the opportunity to work in education and nonprofit organizations within New York City schools, which provided me with valuable insights into the American education system. In the classroom, I focused on honing my skills in developing web applications, gaining hands-on experience with various programming languages and frameworks to create innovative, education-based projects.

Right now, I am working on building an app that is focused on language literacy! The goal is to create a tool that encourages people to practice new languages by writing journal entries. contact me to learn more :)
